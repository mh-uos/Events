Finding your exact Opportunity Types:
Tabs are sorted based on Opportunity Types you define. To find your exact terminology, go to TargetConnect:
Maintenance > Manage Lists > Job Related Lists > Opportunity types and view the 'Description' column. (This is case-insensitive).

Any vacancy that doesn't match a custom tab below will go into this default tab.

How to bypass the 10-item limit

This widget uses a free service called RSS2JSON to translate the messy TargetConnect feed into clean data. By default, they limit anonymous feeds to 10 items to save bandwidth.

  1. Go to rss2json.com and sign up for a free account.
  2. Once logged in, click on "My Account" and navigate to your API Key.
  3. Copy that long string of text.
  4. Check the "Use an API Key" box in this builder and paste it in!

Note: You can secure your key on the RSS2JSON site by restricting it to only work on your specific GitHub Pages and TargetConnect URLs.
